ABOUT US

Welcome to Apartment Frane – Your Tranquil Escape in Istria

Apartment Frane is an ideal retreat where you can unwind in peaceful surroundings, enjoying nature’s beauty with opportunities for walking, horseback riding, berry picking, and biking, all away from traffic and noise. Guests can also participate in seasonal truffle hunting with trained dogs, either individually or in groups, for a unique experience.

Historical Charm and Modern Comfort

Built in 1750, the house is made entirely of stone and was meticulously renovated in 2010 to offer modern comfort while preserving its historical character. As the only guests, you’ll have the entire house to yourselves; the owner does not reside on-site during your stay.

Features of the House

  • Fully equipped kitchen and a cozy living room with a fireplace
  • Three bedrooms (two with double beds and one with two double beds)
  • Bathroom with a shower
  • Air conditioning, heating, satellite TV, and more for your comfort
  • A wine cellar with house-produced wine and brandy for guests to enjoy

Outdoor Space and Amenities

Surrounded by lush greenery, the spacious garden offers:

  • A fireplace with outdoor seating
  • Private outdoor pool
  • Private parking area

Year-Round Availability

The house is open to guests all year round, offering a warm welcome and a perfect base to explore Istria’s beauty in every season.

The Kramar Family Welcomes You

We look forward to hosting you at Apartment Frane and ensuring a pleasant stay filled with relaxation, history, and natural beauty in Istria.
